# 📊 Northwind Sales & Operations Analysis Project



## 🧠 Project Summary

This project delivers a comprehensive analysis of business performance using the Northwind Traders dataset, focusing on sales, customers, products, and operations.

The project was completed using SQL for data extraction and transformation and Power BI for visualization and dashboard development, providing actionable insights for business decision-making.

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Northwind Traders operates across multiple regions with diverse customers, products, and suppliers.

This project answers key business questions:

-What are the company’s revenue trends over time?

-Who are the most valuable customers?

-Which products and categories drive the most revenue?

-How efficient are operations and delivery processes?

-What strategies can improve sales and operational performance?

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

MySQL → Data extraction, transformation, and analysis

Power BI → Data modeling, DAX calculations, and dashboards

CSV Exports → Data transfer between SQL and Power BI

## 📂 Dataset Overview
Database: Northwind Traders

Key Tables Used:

-Orders

-Order Details

-Customers

-Products

-Employees

-Shippers

-Categories

The dataset represents a real-world retail and supply chain system, covering sales transactions, logistics, and customer activity.

## 🧹 Data Preparation (Completed)
Wrote SQL queries to extract and structure required datasets

Performed joins across multiple tables

Created aggregated datasets:

-Revenue by month

-Revenue by category

-Revenue by customer

-Orders by country

Exported results as CSV files for Power BI

## 📊 Analysis Performed

The following analyses were successfully completed:

-Total Revenue and Total Orders

-Average Order Value (AOV)

-Revenue trends (monthly & yearly)

-Top and bottom performing products

-Top customers by revenue

-Revenue contribution by category

-Employee performance analysis

-On-time delivery rate analysis

## 🔍 Key Findings

-A small group of customers contributes a large portion of total revenue

-Certain product categories dominate overall sales performance

-Some products consistently underperform and may require review

-Delivery performance impacts customer satisfaction and repeat business

-Revenue shows seasonal trends across months

## 💡 Business Recommendations

-Focus on retaining and expanding high-value customers

-Optimize inventory by promoting high-performing products

-Review or discontinue low-performing products

-Improve logistics to increase on-time delivery rates

-Leverage seasonal trends for better sales planning
